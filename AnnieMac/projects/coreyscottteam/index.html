Our Team

The Corey Scott Team at AnnieMac Home Mortgage is a team of experienced mortgage professionals. With Corey and his three mortgage loan originators, the Team is able to always step in and get the answers when you need them – even if that means nights and weekends, we’ve got it covered.

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality financial services.  A member of the Corey Scott Team will work with you one on one to ensure that you get a financial solution that is tailored specifically to meet your financing needs. Whether you are purchasing your dream home, refinancing an outstanding loan, or consolidating debt, we can help you find the right loan program.

The ultimate goal is to create lasting relationships with each of our clients so that we may continue providing excellent service for many years to come. The Corey Scott Team looks forward to working with you to achieve your housing goals!

Corey Scott

Branch Manager

Corey grew up in midcoast Maine, Union and went on to earn his Bachelor’s degree from the University of Maine in Business Administration. He first dove into real estate as an agent but quickly learned numbers were more his thing and has been growing his business as a mortgage lender for the past 10 years. In his career, Corey has helped families across Maine, New Hampshire, Massachusetts, Connecticut, North Carolina, Florida, and Rhode Island with over 1800 loans and a total volume now over 425 million. As a mortgage lender, Corey takes pride in making sure clients receive the “Corey Scott Team” service – feeling like each client is his only client and an important part of his business.

Corey, his wife and two young daughters live in Maine. He is very passionate about real estate investing and using it as a tool to build wealth. Helping clients learn about the potential of the market is one of his favorite parts of his role!

Megan Scott

Branch Manager

Megan is a Maine native, having grown up in Augusta and completed her Bachelor’s Degree from the University of Maine in Orono, a BA in Romance Languages (yes, that’s a degree), and MBA from Thomas College in Waterville. Megan was in admissions and international recruiting at Thomas College before jumping into real estate as an agent for many years. After taking a small break to be at home with Megan and Corey’s daughters, Megan hung up her real estate license to join the Corey Scott Team in the lending world.  Megan enjoys real estate investing, a good renovation and decorating home project and is a beach enthusiast who loves all thing summer in Maine, especially with her two girls Adrianna and Mila.

Amanda Carmen

Mortgage Loan Originator

Amanda grew up in Manchester, NH. After high school, she worked in retail, managing the logistics for a large toy store, and then managing the showroom for a jewelry store. She then moved into the hospitality industry, working to help manage a hotel while also raising her young son, Vincent. In 2012, Amanda brought her problem-solving and customer service skills to the mortgage industry. Over the years, Amanda has learned the ins and outs of many aspects of the loan process through various departments. In 2020, a transition to the “front line” having a more direct impact in helping families with their home financing needs as a part of the Corey Scott Team was a great fit!

Amanda grew up in the “big city” and decided to move to a small town in NH along with her husband, Ben, and her son. When she is not working, Amanda loves to spend time with her family and going on hikes with their rescue dog, Buckley. Amanda also volunteers doing home visits and transportation for Rescue Dogs Rock NYC, helping to connect rescue dogs with wonderful new forever homes!

Billie Cook

Mortgage Loan Originator

Billie was born and raised in Southern Maine. She earned her Associate’s degree in Business Administration at York County Technical College and then her Bachelor’s at the University of Southern Maine. Billie worked in veterinary laboratory sales in New York City but moved back home shortly after 9/11 and decided to pursue her passion for food completing her Le Cordon Bleu Diploma at the Atlantic Culinary Academy in Dover, NH. She worked in catering and as a personal chef before deciding to stay home to raise her two boys, Spencer and Ian. Billie returned to the workforce in the mortgage world and has learned the ins and outs of disclosing, compliance and processing over the past 7 years. The Corey Scott Team is lucky to have her as a Mortgage Consultant!

Outside of work, Billie is a registered volunteer with the Boy Scouts of America, loves skiing at Sunday River with her sons and husband, Jeff, and also loves spending time bicycling the Eastern Trail and frequenting the beaches in Southern Maine in warmer months!

Jessica Farnsworth

Mortgage Loan Originator

Jessica grew up in Massachusetts and spent her summers in Maine’s Mid Coast area, which always felt like home, so she moved to Camden in 2003 and began her financial career with MBNA, who later merged and became Bank of America. While working full time she studied Business Administration at The University of Maine. After 8 years with Bank of America Jessica decided to relocate to Southern Maine to be closer to family. At that time she joined the mortgage industry and has enjoyed helping clients achieve home ownership ever since. Jessica enjoys spending her weekends with her two young children, Abigail and Owen.

Mike Rock

Business Development Manager

Michael was born and raised in Westport, Connecticut.  He attended the University of New Hampshire where he earned his Bachelor’s degree in Communication.  Right after graduating from UNH, Michael joined RR Donnelley, a global printing and client communication organization.  During Michael’s 17 year tenure at RRD, he was able to enhance his skills to ensure each and every client had a unique and positive experience.  Michael has always had a passion for real estate and people, so joining the Corey Scott Team in 2021 to cultivate relationships was a perfect fit.

Outside of work, Michael enjoys spending time outside with his wife, Jessica and two children, Carter and Amelia.

Why Work With Us?

We truly care about what is best for you! From pre-approval to closing, our goal is to provide you with the best service while saving you money. We provide a completely customized experience, and will find the right loan program and best terms for you.

The most important part of our success is our clients and real estate agents who trust us every day to educate and advocate for them in the mortgage process. We have multiple loan products, giving our clients more options, competitive rates, and low fees. We are passionate about providing great customer service.
