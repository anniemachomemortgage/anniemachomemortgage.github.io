Search Results

  • Processing and Underwriting

    Mortgage processing is when your personal financial information is collected and analyzed to ensure that all proper documentation is in place before the loan paperwork is sent to underwriting. Underwriting is a thorough evaluation of your personal financial information and supporting documentation by a licensed underwriter.

  • Loan Interest Rates

    An interest rate is the percentage of your existing principal loan balance you pay your lender in exchange for borrowing money to purchase a home. The interest rate is used to calculate your monthly mortgage payment. The higher the interest rate, the higher your monthly payment will be.

  • The Steps to Refinancing

    Refinancing your mortgage is simply the process of receiving a new loan for your home. When you refinance your current home loan, the new mortgage loan pays off the old one, leaving you with one loan and one monthly payment.

  • Prequal & Preappoval Steps

    Pre-qualification is one of the initial steps in your home buying journey, usually done prior to beginning the home-buying process. Mortgage pre-approval is as close as you can get to confirming your credit and financial stability without having a contract in place. Once you complete a mortgage application, your lender will verify the information you provide and conduct a credit check.
